WE WOULD LOVE YOU TO KNOW WORKING PRINCIPLES

Lawyers and legal assistants of the firm represent clients in judicial and commercial arbitration procedures, all types of courts and in all instances. We assist in debt collection, dispute settlement in the field of employment legal relations, disputes arising from execution of economic transactions, administrative disputes, and in other legal matters.

In addition to judicial procedures, our lawyers can also recommend mediation process. Judicial mediation is a dispute settlement procedure aimed at assisting parties in civil cases at reaching an amicable dispute resolution with the help of one or several mediators.

Our lawyers and their assistants can prepare commercial transactions, subsequent agreements and represent the client in negotiations with contractors either in both presence of absence of the client.

In the professional activities, our lawyers follow the principles and standards established in the Law on the Bar of the Republic of Lithuania:
freedom and independence of lawyers’ activities;
democracy, collegiality and fair competition in mutual relations between lawyers;
lawfulness of lawyer's activities;
non-disclosure of client's secrets;
loyalty to the client and avoidance of conflict of interests;
impeccable behaviour.
